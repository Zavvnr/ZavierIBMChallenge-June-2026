# ⚽ MlangCast Multilingual Tactical Explainers

## Disclaimer
This project was built as part of the IBM SkillsBuild AI Builders June Challenge. The challenge takes inspiration from the 2026 FIFA World Cup tournament.

## 🚀 Project Title & Overview
The FIFA Men World Cup event is the most anticipated event on Earth with an estimation about billions of people watching each time the tournament is held. The experience and expectations vary for each country, yet they aim for the same goal, that is to win the world cup trophy. Each country come to the world cup with their own strength and weaknesses, leading to different tactical implementation in the field.

In order to fill in the excitement for those streaming the matches from home, football commentaries have been a standard in some countries that stream the world cup. Commentaries are the ones narating the game so people watching from home are aware of the "what" by listening to the narative brought by commentaries.

Despite the narration brought by commentaries, they still come with one problem. That is, they don't come with every native language that exist.

**MlangCast Tactical Explainer** *(MTE)* comes to address the problem. It is an AI-Agent that will bring more "humane" experience to the people around the world, by filling the gap caused by the absence of commentary in some languages. While it is certainly possible to engineer AI-Agents that comment on the movements of the ball, an extra analysis tool is required for a tactical explainer that will be able to ensure that the agents correctly identify each event and analyzes factors that corelates to the particular event. For example, in the world cup context, it might be the case that a gap that is open led to a goal.

Combining two challenge themes:
- **Understanding & Explanation** – AI match explainers, tactical shift analysis, momentum visualization tools.
- **Fan & Learning Experiences** – Personalized AI World Cup companions, multilingual tactical explainers, “teach me the game” assistants.

MTE will create an ultimate experience for those watching without a native language commentary.
